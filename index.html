FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you DJ?

I specialize in weddings, corporate events, private parties, and special celebrations. If your event needs music, hosting, and good energy, I’m in.

Will you also MC and make announcements?

Yes. I’m happy to handle all key announcements—grand entrance, toasts, first dances, cake cutting, bouquet toss, and more—so your timeline flows smoothly.

Can we choose our own music?

Absolutely. I’ll ask you for “must-play” songs, genres you love, and a “do-not-play” list. From there, I build a custom set that fits your style and keeps the dance floor full.

Do you take song requests from guests?

Yes, as long as they fit your preferences and the vibe of the event. I always prioritize the couple’s or host’s wishes first.

What equipment do you bring?

I provide a full professional sound system, DJ controller, microphones, and basic dance floor lighting. I also bring backup gear so the music doesn’t stop.

How long do you usually play?

Most packages cover 4–6 hours of performance time. Additional hours can be added if you know your crowd will want to dance late into the night.
